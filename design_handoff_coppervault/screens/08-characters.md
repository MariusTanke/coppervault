# 08 · Characters

## Purpose
Dramatis personae browser.

## Layout
- Header `CVKicker(s.dramatis)` + Display `s.characters`
- Search bar: `CVInput(s.searchChars, icon=search, height=38)`
- Section: `CVKicker(s.worldhoppers)`
- Grid of 2 cols, gap 10 dp. Each card:
  - 48 dp avatar circle (radial gradient from `accent` to `void`)
  - Name (Display 14)
  - Role (mono 9 sp `Ash` uppercase)
  - World chip (accent border-left)

## Data
At least: Hoid, Khriss, Nazh, Galladon, Demoux, Vasher/Zahel, Felt.
Then by world: Kaladin, Shallan, Dalinar, Szeth (Roshar); Vin, Kelsier, Wax, Wayne (Scadrial); Vivenna, Siri (Nalthis); Raoden, Sarene (Sel).
